The purpose of the platform is not to learn every word in the script. Rather, it is to: learn to understand the drama with the least amount of effort possible.

Principles:
  - Words that cannot be easily separated should not be separated. They should be ignored or left in a combined form
  - Complicated grammar should not be explained. Instead, they should be ignored or left in a combined form. Examples:
    - Particles (ha, ga, ni, etc..) (left combined or ignored)
    - Conjugation (eba, atta, etc..) (left combined)
    - verb combinations (left combined)
    - etc...
  - Users will be able to ask and answer questions about specific sentences through the platform. This is a form of higher level learning than a game can provide.
  - Simple and often repeated words should not be repeatedly learned. Complicated and infrequent words should be relearned.

Reasons:
  - It is hard to understand complicated concepts with a simple game
